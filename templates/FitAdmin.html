What's a good beginner workout for weight loss?

Here's a simple beginner workout plan focusing on weight loss:

1. Cardio: 20-30 minutes of brisk walking or cycling

2. Strength: Bodyweight squats (3 sets of 10-12)

3. Core: Plank (3 sets, hold for 20-30 seconds)

Do this 3-4 times per week, gradually increasing intensity.

How many calories does this burn?

A typical session burns approximately 200-300 calories, depending on your weight and intensity level. Remember that diet is equally important for weight loss.
